Hack the North

Designing for Canada's biggest hackathon.

background

Hack the North is Canada's largest hackathon held at the University of Waterloo. We bring over 1000 students from over 22 countries to build and create over a period of 36 hours.

For the past two years, I was a part of the Hack the North organizing team as a Product Designer. I've worked on various projects such as the mobile app, the volunteer dashboard, and other various assets that was needed for the event.

2018 Website Animation

Every year the Hack the North team changes our branding. We wanted to be very different from previous years so we decided to stray away from the monochromatic blue branding to something more colourful and as well as include illustrations. This year, Gracie Xia, an excellent illustrator and designer lead the illustrations for 2018's branding. She lead the website hero's illustration and while I illustrated some minor parts of the hero such as the robot or people and as well as being in charge of animating the illustration on Adobe After Effects. Check out the live version of the animation here on our website.
