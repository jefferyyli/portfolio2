Summer 2019

During the summer of 2019, I worked at Twitter as a Product Design Intern. I was a part of the Creative Product Team on the Design & Research org. From there I assisted with touching on different parts of the app and as well as contributing towards Twitter's new design system.

Collaborated with a motion designer to create a pull-to-refresh animation for our new visual language

My work mainly focused around visual and interaction design. Due to NDA, I cannot share my work publicly. If interested, please reach out to me to view some of the work I've done at Twitter.

Some highlights working at Twitter were: meeting Jack and the executive team, random celebrity spottings like Lil Nas X at the NYC office, flying out to San Francisco both times for orientation and connecting with others interns, and for Twitter Design day! And of course, living in New York City itself was an amazing experience. It was definitely a fun and memorable summer at Twitter!
